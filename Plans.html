Our Facilities

The accommodation area is surrounded by farm on all sides, with plantation as well. Our agro resort is surrounded by Sahyadri range of mountains and exotic malshej ghat views. A well designed ambience and a touch of nature views are what set our rooms apart, combining the ethnic characteristics of a cultural environment with a high level of comfort and sophistication. We provide rooms with highest comfort and facilities on a hand. With pleasant environs and breath-taking views we boost your private space rooming with ultimately best living and refreshments.

Each of these Luxury Farm Villas offers peace and quiet environment that you can enjoy you from your expansive, humdrum living area. It is a heaven in the country offering serenity, heavenly landscapes and the rich heritage of the land – an ideal family retreat or honeymoon hideaway.

Our variety of Indoor Games are for those who want to make the most of their vacation. The Indoor games at Malshej include Carrom, Table Tennis, Snooker and many more.
